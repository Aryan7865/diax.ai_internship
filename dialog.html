Welcome to Dialog

It is the application for hospitals/clinics/practices, used by medical services and allied personnels like doctors, nurses, attendants, pharmacist and lab technicians.

Doctors

Timing

Doctors can set and adjust their weekly consultation timings, including times off or unavailability, for patients to use when booking appointments.

Consultation

This application's primary goal is to simplify prescriptions. Diary recommends suitable medications, which doctors can adjust to meet each patient's unique needs.

Pick Appointment Slot

Doctors can view all their appointments in a calendar based on their set timings, allowing effective time management.

Reports

Default reports are available based on appointments and diagnoses. Custom reports can be created with full support provided.

No location barriers

Book in-clinic or video consultation appointments. Video consultations let you connect with your doctor from anywhere.

Hospital/Clinic/Practice

Users

Apart from doctors, nurses and attendants are key personnel at a medical center. Mostly, these resources work in shifts.

The application provides an efficient way to manage shift schedule for these resources. User can be added/removed, roles updated for restrictive or administrative access for efficent functioning of the center.
