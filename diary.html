Welcome to Diary

Your Personal Health Companion

Whether you're navigating chronic health conditions, pursuing a more vibrant lifestyle, or simply seeking to stay well-informed about your overall well-being, Diary provides the comprehensive support and insights you need.

Diary is designed to help you manage your health and wellness journey with ease. Here are some key features:

Metrics

Easily monitor your body weight, height , waist circumference and BMI( Body Mass Index) signs over time for better health management.

Vitals Tracker

Track health metrics such as blood glucose, insulin dose, blood pressure, pulse, SpO2, temperature and insulin titration is possible with the help of appropriate vitals.

Diet Tracker

Keep track of your daily food intake, portion sizes, and any dietary remarks and upload your images of food that you consume to track your diet.

Sleep Tracker

Use Sleep Tracker to manage your sleep. Record your sleep start and end times, along with sleep duration, for better sleep management.

Fitness

Regularly monitor and log your exercise routines and physical activities to maintain peak fitness, stay active, and reach your health goals.

Appointments

A calendar view provides an easy to view method for all your appointments. Never miss an appointment with our automated alerts system.

Appointments can be cancelled or changed to a different date as needed. The booking can be either for physical or digital appointments.

My Bills

Diary also acts as a repository for all your medical bills or invoices. It is now easier to view invoices that are de-cluttered and saved by data.

The invoices can also be uploaded or directly saved from connected applications. Track your spend using reports from invoices and take smart decisions.
