Welcome to Diary - Your Personal Health Companion

Diary is designed to help you manage your health and wellness journey with ease. Here are some key features:

Metrics

Track vital health metrics such as blood glucose, insulin dose, blood pressure, pulse, SpO2, and temperature.

Vitals Tracker

Easily record and monitor your vital signs over time for better health management.

Food

Log your dietary intake and meal timings to maintain a balanced diet.

Diet Tracker

Keep track of your daily food intake, portion sizes, and any dietary remarks.

Sleep

Monitor your sleep patterns and duration to improve sleep quality and overall well-being.

Sleep Tracker

Record your sleep start and end times, along with sleep duration, for better sleep management.

Fitness

Track your exercise routines and physical activities to stay fit and active.

Exercise Tracker

Log your exercise sessions, including type, duration, and frequency, to monitor fitness progress.

Whether you're managing chronic conditions, striving for a healthier lifestyle, or simply want to stay informed about your well-being, Diary has you covered.

Appointments

A calendar view provides an easy to view method for all your appointments. Never miss an appointment with our automated alerts system.

Appointments can be cancelled or changed to a different date as needed. The booking can be either for physical or digital appointments.

My Bills

Diary also acts as a repository for all your medical bills or invoices. It is now easier to view invoices that are de-cluttered and saved by data.

The invoices can also be uploaded or directly saved from connected applications. Track your spend using reports from invoices and take smart decisions.

Digital Health Wallet

Transform your medical record management with our secure, centralized storage solution.

Say goodbye to cumbersome paper records and hello to convenient access from anywhere, at any time. With our platform, your entire medical history is at your fingertips, ensuring seamless communication with healthcare providers and eliminating the risk of overlooking vital information.

Wellness

Diary isn't just a healthcare app - it's your personalized wellness companion.

Seamlessly track and monitor your overall well-being through a range of essential parameters, including sleep patterns, dietary habits, exercise routines, and even lifestyle choices like good and bad habits.

Dashboard

Complementing all of these features is the dashboard. A single pictorial/chart view of complete, upto date health data.

Make, take and be responsible for all your health conditions. Self-awareness, self-knowledge about one-self leads to healthy and happy living.

Chats

Chat with doctors and other medical personnel real-time and get all your queries clarified instantly

No need to disappoint anyone in the queue waiting to visit. A path to peace of mind, leading to healthy life.
