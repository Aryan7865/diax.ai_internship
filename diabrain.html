Patterns

Drugs are prescribed after diagnosis and based on lab report indicators. Patterns in prescriptions by population allow for efficient, time-saving automation.

The CDSS helps doctors prescribe drugs based on lab results and vitals. Doctors can consent, change, or remove these recommendations and add new drugs suited to the patient's current condition.

The CDSS saves doctors time, allowing them to consult more patients effectively. Analyzing patient data for ADR detection helps identify and prevent potential adverse reactions, improving medication safety.

Intelligence

Analyzing long-term data provides key insights into ailments, allowing the extraction of patterns and assessment of drug effectiveness.

Machine learning and AI technologies help deduce patterns and refine drug models for effective patient care. For diabetic care, 20 years of validated patient data are used to extract insights.

AI algorithms in medical imaging analyze X-rays, MRIs, and CT scans to assist in diagnosing diseases. Deep learning detects abnormalities, improves accuracy, and supports radiologists in image interpretation.
